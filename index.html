Our Specialties

The Process

Our process is simple, at any given point in your day you are only 4 steps from a delicious bowl of your favorite Cereals: Step 1) Go to Cereals. Step 2) Choose from one of our luxurious premium Cereals. Step 3) Fill your bowl with your choice from our array of Dairy or Non-Dairy Milks and Milk Alternatives. Step 3) Allow us to adorn your combination with your choice of Toppings!

1. CEREAL

Nutritious Homemade Granola

Recipes made with combinations of honey, graham crackers, roasted cinnimon, and more...

Premium Quality Cereals

All of our Cereals are GMO and Gluten Free, Vegan, and of course... Delicious!

Sweet, Warm Oatmeal and More

Whole grain Oatmeal, Cream of Wheat, Grits, and other Weekly Specialialty Items.

2. MILK

Dairy Milk

Good, Old Fashioned Whole to Skim Dairy Cow and Goat's milk

Dairy Free Options

Soy Milk, Freshly Pressed Almond, Cashew, and other delicious non-dairy Milk alternatives.

Milk Tea and other Alternatives

Fill your bowl with Iced Milk Teas, Smoothies, Horchata, and other exotic combinations for your Cereals.

3. TOPPINGS

Nuts

Choose a combination of energy-packed Almonds, Cashews, Walnuts, and other salty favorites.

Fresh Fruit and Berries

Enjoy delicious, nutritious, fruits and berries choices like strawberry, banana, mango, and kiwi.

Sinful Sweets

Toppings of all flavors like gummys, oreo's, boba tea bubbles, crumbled toasted tarts, decadant syrups, and more!
